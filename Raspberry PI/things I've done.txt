editere /etc/network/interfaces og legg til

iface wlan0 inet manual
wpa-roam /etc/wpa_supplicant/wpa_supplicant.conf
iface default inet dhcp

edit /etc/wpa_supplicant/wpa_supplicant.conf:
network={
  ssid="yourssid"
  psk="yourpassword"
}
